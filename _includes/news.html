With great amount of storage, comes great amount of XCH. Chia crypto with Host Managed SMR.

Drachma - world's first Host-Managed SMR crypto-storage system for Chia farming. Chia users can finally take advantage of the huge capacity of HM-SMR devices and avoid the need to replace SSDs. This means more plots and more margin for your Chia farm.


More storage, more Chia.

Chia is a new cryptocurrency where mining work is based on storage capacity (proof of space) rather than processing (proof of work). However, its popularity is quickly straining the supply of storage devices and the mining work can quickly destroy even enterprise-grade SSDs.

 

Chia farming, simplified.

Drachma makes Chia farming easy and efficient. Using high capacity HM-SMR drives, we dramatically reduced the amount of space and energy consumed for Chia farming. Having fewer drives also means fewer component failures and lower maintenance costs.
