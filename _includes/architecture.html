Phalanx is the storage engine of Olympus. It is designed and optimized for software-define environments and next generation storage devices. Phalanx's novel row/column architecture, device-aware design and log structured data layout minimize contention and latency while preventing hot write areas. Phalanx can also be easily containerized and deployed using existing orchestration frameworks (e.g. Kubernetes).
